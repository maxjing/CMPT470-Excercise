According to Wikipedia, there was never a version 1.4 of HTML. The W3C is the World Wide Web Consortium, and it defines HTML.

HTML5 History

  1. October 28, 2014 HTML5[24] was published as a W3C Recommendation.[25]
  2. November 1, 2016 HTML 5.1[26] was published as a W3C Recommendation.[27][28]
  3. December 14, 2017 HTML 5.2[29] was published as a W3C Recommendation.[30][31]
